💡 The #1 finding: a "link" vs "url" wording mismatch. Thousands of users search "jpg to link", "image to link", "gif to link" — but our pages are titled "...to URL". Google still ranks us (often page 1), yet our snippet says the "wrong" word, so almost nobody clicks. "jpg to link" alone: 1,462 impressions, ranking #8.9, but only 1.1% CTR (16 clicks). Adding "link" to titles/H1s is a one-line edit that could ~5–10× clicks on these.
