The story in one line: a young site growing fast on the back of a strong non-English localization strategy (89% of clicks are non-brand). Two things are capping it: (1) we rank but get few clicks because page titles say "URL" while users type "link"; (2) we're nearly invisible in the big Western markets (US/UK/DE) where we sit on page 7. Most upside needs zero new pages — just title/snippet fixes and authority.
