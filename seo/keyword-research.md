## Competitor gap (image2url.com)

The competitor ranks for ~1,000 keywords. Beyond the core image-to-url cluster they also rank for **mp3-to-url**, **qr-code**, and **gif-compression** tools we don't offer — ignore those (different products). The actionable overlap is the **core image-to-url cluster + "image as link" + "how to get image url"**, where they are top 11 and we are absent or buried. Closing those three clusters is the highest-leverage work.

## Audit findings (homepage, instant on-page audit)

- **Title 66 chars** — flagged too long (trim to ≤60, front-load "Image to URL").
- **Meta description 184 chars** — too long (trim to ~150–160).
- **H1** "Image to URL in  One Click" — contains a **double space** typo; keep the keyword, fix the spacing.
- **`low_content_rate`** flag — text-to-HTML ratio low (ad + markup heavy). Fix by adding useful copy, **not** by removing ads (hard rule).
- TTFB ~1.9s — known Cloudflare JSD + `/`→`/en/` redirect cost; out of scope for this on-page pass.
- Positives: canonical present, HTTPS, favicon, 0 images missing alt, 1,877 words, 113 internal links, FAQ JSON-LD present.

## Cluster coverage analysis (2026-06-07, added)

~30 real on-intent clusters around the main target (excluding the competitor's non-image tools: mp3-to-url, qr-code, audio — noise for us). Of ~480 classifiable on-intent keywords (vol ≥300), **443 (~92% of volume) now have a canonical page** after this session's work. Remaining genuine gaps (37 kw / ~350k vol):

| Gap cluster | Combined vol | KD | Action | Canonical note |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| **GIF compression** (gif compressor, compress a gif, reduce gif size, animated gif compressor…) | ~150,000 | 10–17 | NEW page `gif-compressor` (needs client-side WASM gif encoder) | distinct from generic `image-compressor` |
| **Image converter hub** (image converter, online image converter, free image converter) | ~35,000 | 38–63 | NEW hub page `image-converter` linking to specific converters | hub intent, not a format pair — no cannibalization |
| **jpg vs jpeg** (jpg vs jpeg, is jpg and jpeg the same) | ~16,000 | 3–17 | fold into existing `/guides/jpg-vs-png-vs-webp-vs-avif` or `jpeg-to-jpg` FAQ | consolidate, don't make a thin duplicate |
| Niche (bmp to jpg, black-and-white converter) | ~3,000 | 0–15 | optional small `bmp-to-jpg` + grayscale tool | low priority |

**Canonical-safety rule (important):** most "missing" keywords are *synonyms* of owned intents (`pic url`, `url picture`, `image to link`, `images to link`, `image size converter`). These must be **consolidated** into the existing page's H1 variants / intro / FAQ — NOT given their own page, or two of our URLs cannibalize one query and Google discounts one. Only **distinct, unmet intent** earns a new page. The site already maps one canonical URL per intent (root stub → `/en/`); preserve that 1:1.
